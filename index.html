ASSAM AND BIHAR FLOOD RELIEF

With Brahmaputra and eight other rivers flowing above danger levels, the state of Assam and Bihar were battling two disasters of flood and the pandemic. The situation in the state continued to deteriorate and our help was needed to ensure displaced families get immediate relief.
